Purpose

This simulation demonstrates how businesses use Business Intelligence (BI) and Decision Support Systems (DSS) to make informed decisions based on data.

Key Concepts

  • Operational Decisions: Use the latest data (real-time conditions)
  • Strategic Decisions: Use trends (patterns over time)
  • Sales and inventory must be thought in a relation
  • Decisions depend on current conditions and plans for the future

Key Lessons

  • ✔ Last month data reflects what is happening NOW
  • ✔ Trends (3 months) show direction and patterns
  • ✔ High demand + low inventory → restock immediately
  • ✔ Low demand + high inventory → increase marketing
  • ✔ Using averages alone can hide urgent problems
  • ✔ Bad decisions come from misreading data
  • ✔ Every action has operational consequences

Insight

“Use the latest data to ACT, and trend data to PLAN.”
“Data does not make decisions, people do. Better data leads to better decisions.”
