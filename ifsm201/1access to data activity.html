When Information Is Instant, Why Should People (🫵 You) Need to Learn?

If all information is instantly available, do people still need to learn or memorize anything?
If you can get answers instantly… how do you know they’re correct, useful, or safe?
What must you know already before using search or AI effectively?
Instant access to information doesn’t eliminate the need to learn.

It raises the cost of not understanding, because without thinking skills, you cannot detect mistakes, judge consequences, or act responsibly.
