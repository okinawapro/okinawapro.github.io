Instructional Context: Understanding Packet Analysis

In this module, we are moving beyond abstract models and looking at the data that traverses a network wire. Every packet shares a fundamental anatomy: it is encapsulated, meaning smaller payloads are wrapped inside outer delivery headers.

As security analysts, we perform Deep Packet Inspection (DPI). This allows us to spot anomalies, structural weaknesses, and information leaks. Click on each packet in the capture stream below to analyze its layers, study its associated security vector, and test your knowledge.
