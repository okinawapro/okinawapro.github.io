Lessons Learned

Testing exists to protect users and systems from hidden failure modes. Each test type targets a specific risk. When tests are skipped or rushed, problems escape into production where they become exponentially more expensive and harmful. In critical systems, inadequate testing is not a delay — it is a liability.
