Key Takeaway

A SWOT analysis is not about labeling traits — it is about building strategic self‑awareness that leads to action.
By clearly separating what you control (strengths and weaknesses) from what you must respond to (opportunities and threats), you gain power over your decisions.

SWOT shifts thinking from “What do I have?” to “I will 🏃‍➡️...”
