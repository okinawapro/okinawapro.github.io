🛡️ What Does “11 Nines” Mean?

11 nines durability means data loss is statistically extremely unlikely — less than one object lost per 100,000 years for large datasets. Cloud providers achieve this through automatic replication across multiple servers and locations.

This does not mean downloads are free or fast — cloud storage is extremely durable, but moving data can be expensive.
